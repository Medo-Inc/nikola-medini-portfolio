Olival Job Application

About the project

This project was a task for a job application company called Olival. I was required to create promotional material for their Sun product line. Promotional material consisted of an Instagram Story ad, a social media visual, and a hero banner.
First, I began drawing ideas on paper. My main motif was the sun, which inspired me to design a sun pattern. I sketched a couple of sun patterns and invisoned the colors of the promotarial to be shades of the setting sun.

Color Palette

Elemetns used in pattern

Pattern

I started by drawing a couple of suns to serve as patterns. After deciding on the colors, I designed a pattern that I used as the background for the promotional materials.
I used Helivetica font for its clarity and added shadows beneath the text to reflect the sun's steady fading from yellow to crimson as it sets.
For the story ad, I animated the pattern to make the design feel more alive.

Story Ad and Promotional Visual

Hero Banner

I utilized the same pattern for the hero banner, but inverted the colors and added SUN products in front of the background.
I wanted to show how many and what things are on sale, giving the impression that there are numerous products to be purchased.

Promotional Poster

My next work was to design a promotional poster for Olival deo sticks. First, I wanted to make a poster depicting deo sticks strolling down the runway, with each color of carpet representing their aroma.
However, after much trial and error a idea was born, that I decided to adopt as a final proposal. Similar to the original design, I divided the poster into three identical sizes and utilized photographs of different smells as backdrops to highlight their smell.
