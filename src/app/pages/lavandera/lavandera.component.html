About the SEO strategy

I started by investigating rival websites and evaluating how they appeared in Google search results. In order to promote my website with distinctive keywords, I looked into their descriptions and titles for phrases that weren't utilized frequently. I used the most common keywords in the description that people would use when searching for laundry services (preuzimanje, pranje, su≈°enje, peglanje, besplatna dostava rublja). For the title, I used unique keywords like the name of the company, Lavandera, and the region from where they are operating, Makarska. As a result, they can easily be at the top in their region.
A website's introduction reduces bounce rates since it quickly grabs users' attention. The website loads quickly because it isn't overly bloated and is hence lightweight, which is advantageous for SEO.
