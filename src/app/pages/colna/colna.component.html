About the project

In this project I had to make wine lable concept and design for the client. First I had to come up with ideas, I wanted the label to look as modern and minimalistic as possible. The simplicity and clarity of a single grape came to mind first.

Then I decided to experiment a little.

Final Result

Finally, I decided that the final concept was the best because it represented the harsh dalmatian winds and their impact on wine production.
